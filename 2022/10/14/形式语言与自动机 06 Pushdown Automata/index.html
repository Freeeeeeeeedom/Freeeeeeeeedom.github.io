Pushdown Automata

下推自动机，表达能力和上下文无关语言等价

Definition

  • The PDA is an automaton equivalent to the CFG in language-defining power 下推自动机的语言定义能力和上下文无关文法相同
  • Only the nondeterministic PDA defines all the CFL' s
    • 仅非确定下推自动机定义所有的上下文无关语言
  • But the deterministic version models parsers
    • Most programing languages have deterministic PDA' s

Intuition: PDA

  • Think of an -NFA with the additional power that can manipulate a stack
  • Its moves are determined by:
    • The current state
    • The current input symbol and
    • The current symbol on top of its stack

Picture of a PDA

Intuition: PDA

  • Being nondeterministic, the PDA can have a choice of next moves
  • In each choice, the PDA can:
    • Change state, and also
    • Replace the top symbol on the stack by a sequence of zero or more symbols
      • Zero symbols = "pop"
      • Many symbols = sequence of "pushes"
